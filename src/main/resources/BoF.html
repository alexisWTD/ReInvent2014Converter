GA106 - Women in Technology Luncheon Please join us for a networking luncheon and panel discussion with Amazon Web Services employees and customers moderated by Teresa Carlson, Vice President of Amazon Web Services Public Sector. While organizations often struggle in relating diversity investment directly to business impact, “diversity of thought” brings us closer to the connection between talent and business results Diversity of thought manifests as differences in the way people approach problems, communicate, and collaborate.  We know from experience and research that cultural and gender diversity are two critical sources for diversity of thought. In this session, we will hear from Amazon Web Services employees and partners about their individual experiences with diversity of thought and its impact on innovation. Please join us to hear about their experiences and to share your own! 90 mintues General Activities Teresa Carlson – Vice President with Amazon Web Services
