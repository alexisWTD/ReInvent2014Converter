This session discusses how Fidelity Investments use Amazon S3 with server-side encryption with customer-provided keys (SSE-C) to protect critical information and the firm's use of other AWS services, which include AWS Elastic Beanstalk, Elastic Load Balancer, and Amazon DynamoDB. FIN303 - Inlet: Leveraging AWS to Change the Way Businesses Communicate with Consumers Broadridge Financial Solutions and Pitney Bowes have partnered to develop a new venture, Inlet, that enables brands across industries to deliver sensitive client communications (such as bills, statements, and tax documents) to the digital channels that consumers use every day, including online banking websites, social media, and cloud storage solutions. Robert Krugman, SVP Digital Strategy, discusses Inlet's service architecture and the competitive advantages and cost benefits of deploying on AWS.     45 minutes Breakout Session Robert Krugman – SVP Digital Strategy with Broadridge Financial Solutions, Inc.
FIN401 - Seismic Shift: Nasdaq's Migration to Amazon Redshift Jason Timmes led the migration of the primary data warehouse for Nasdaq’s Transaction Services U.S. business unit (which operates Nasdaq’s U.S. equity and options exchanges) from a traditional on-premises MPP database to Amazon Redshift. The project significantly reduced operational expenses. Jason, who is an Associate Vice President of Software Development at Nasdaq, describes how his team migrated a warehouse that loads approximately 7 billion rows a day into the cloud, satisfied several security and regulatory audits, optimized read and write performance, ensures high availability, and orchestrates other back-office activities that depend on the warehouse daily loads completing. Along with sharing several technical lessons learned, Jason will discuss Nasdaq’s roadmap to integrating Redshift with more AWS services, as well as with more Nasdaq products, to offer even greater benefit to clients (internal and external) in the months ahead. 45 minutes Breakout Session Jason Timmes – Associate VP of Software Development with Nasdaq OMX
